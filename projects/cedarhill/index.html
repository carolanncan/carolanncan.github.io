City of Cedar Hill

The city is often referred to as “the hill country of Dallas”. It has vast areas of tree cover, a state park with camping grounds, and access to a one of the largest lakes in the region for boating and fishing.

The subdued colors and overlapping geometric elements give the concept a modern feel that would appeal to young and old demographics alike.

The visitor's guide contains information on local attractions which are mainly the dense tree cover in the city and other outdoors features.

The city advertises in the Dallas Lovefield airport, whose abbreviation is “LUV”. We wanted to play on the abbreviation in our banner for travelers.
