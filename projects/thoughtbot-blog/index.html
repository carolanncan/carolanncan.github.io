User-centered typography

Here's what I learned:

  • Text is an interface.

    I wrote a blog post about treating text as an interface and how that influenced this design.

  • If you focus on providing value to people first, the rest will follow.

    Optimizing for your content to be share-able and generate traffic isn't always the right place to start. Optimize for a great experience by supplying content people want and creating a good environment for them to read it in.

  • The devil is in the details.

    Every choice regarding scale, space, and color, matters when text is your interface. Nothing beats well-executed typography and composition.
