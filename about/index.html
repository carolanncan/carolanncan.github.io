What I've learned about design

Design is a team sport.

My role as a designer isn't to have all the answers but to combine my expertise with the that of everyone around me.

Start by asking questions.

Ask "why" as many times as you have to in order to get to the root of a problem or the true reason behind a decision.

Communication is critical.

Being able to explain the "why" behind a decision helps everyone around you contribute to the design process and work together toward the same goal.
