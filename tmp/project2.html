Laybull

Facilitating Research through Machine Learning

Playing off of the word "label", Laybull is an application that turns the "lay" person into a powerful tool for machine learning and artificial intelligence. I partenered with a developer who originated the idea for this app. We think we have created an extremely efficient workflow for a very cumbersome task.

Our next step is to begin user-testing on our prototype.
